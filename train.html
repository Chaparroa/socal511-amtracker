Set up alerts on your iPhone

Apple requires websites to be added to your Home Screen before push notifications can work. It's a one-time setup that takes about 30 seconds.

  1. 1
    Tap the Share button in Safari The icon at the bottom of your screen — a box with an arrow pointing up
  2. 2
    Tap "Add to Home Screen" Scroll down in the share menu if you don't see it right away
  3. 3
    Open Amtracker from your Home Screen Tap the new icon that appears on your home screen
  4. 4
    Tap 🔔 and allow notifications You'll get a push alert whenever Train 770 is running late
